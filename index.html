Our Mission

Transparent Henry County is a non-partisan organization dedicated to making local government more accessible. We believe that an informed community is a stronger community — and that starts with making it easier to follow the conversations happening in your county.

We share recordings of county meetings, provide candidate information ahead of elections, and offer tools like our THCA chatbot to help residents quickly find what they need.
